About Us

Xylo is a leave management system is an
all-in-one platform that handles all employee
vacation requests while ensuring smooth
functioning of their operations. It eliminates
paperwork, removes manual interventions.

Xylo offers settings for flexible timings and
shifts. Different holiday sets, shift timings
and weekly-off schedules can be set according
to department.
